SHARING SUNDAY

Introducing"Sharing Sunday" Uniting hearts, Empowering change

In the heart of Malmo Sweden, a beacon compassion shines bright as high school students rally together to create a remarkable charity event: Sharing Friday. Born from a collective desire to create difference in the community.
At Sharing Friday the door swings open wide, welcoming one and all to partake in an extraordinary opportunity to extend a helping hand.

Donate used items or yearning to lend your time and energy as a volunteer!

"Sharing Friday is more than just a collection of donations or volunteers; it is a testament to the incredible power of community spirit. It is a reminder that when individuals come together with a common purpose, they can move mountains and transform."

So, mark your calendars and spread the word!
