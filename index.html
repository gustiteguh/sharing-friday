In the heart of Malmo Sweden, a beacon compassion shines bright as high school students rally together to create a remarkable charity event: Sharing Friday. Born from a collective desire to create difference in the community.
At Sharing Friday the door swings open wide, welcoming one and all to partake in an extraordinary opportunity to extend a helping hand.
